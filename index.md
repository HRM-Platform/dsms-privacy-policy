## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **DSMS Mobile** (Driving School Management System) ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using DSMS Mobile, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.  
This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the DSMS Mobile app, including students, instructors, and administrators. By continuing to use DSMS Mobile after updates are posted, you agree to the revised terms.

---

## Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

1. **Information you provide directly**, and  
2. **Information collected automatically through your device.**

### 1. Personal Information

When you use DSMS Mobile, we may collect the following types of personal information:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and authentication credentials.
- **Profile Information:** User profile details, role (student/instructor/admin), and preferences.
- **Identity Documents:** Driver's license, learner's permit, ID cards, and related documentation.
- **Location Data:** Geographic location information for tracking driving lessons and routes.
- **Images:** Profile photos, document scans, and lesson-related images.
- **Lesson Data:** Driving lesson schedules, progress records, instructor feedback, and attendance.
- **Vehicle Information:** Vehicle details assigned for driving lessons.
- **Payment Information:** Transaction records and payment history (if applicable).

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data:** App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction logs.
- **Network Information:** IP address, connection type, and performance metrics.
- **GPS Data:** Real-time location during active driving lessons.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **To Manage Driving Lessons:** Schedule lessons, track progress, and assign instructors.
- **To Operate and Improve the Service:** Manage accounts, enable features, and enhance user experience.
- **To Track Lesson Routes:** Monitor driving routes for training and safety purposes.
- **To Communicate:** Send notifications about lessons, reminders, and updates.
- **To Ensure Safety:** Track driving sessions, record lesson history, and maintain safety records.
- **To Generate Reports:** Provide progress reports, attendance records, and performance analytics.
- **To Ensure Security and Compliance:** Detect fraud, enforce terms, and comply with legal requirements.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

We securely store limited data locally on your device, such as:

- User preferences and settings.
- Cached data for offline functionality.
- Authentication tokens (encrypted and refreshed periodically).

### Server-Side Protection

Data transmitted to or stored on our servers is protected through:

- **Encryption:** All data sent between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.
- **Access Control:** Restricted access to authorized personnel only.
- **Security Audits:** Regular monitoring and updates to maintain system security.
- **Document Security:** Uploaded documents are encrypted at rest and in transit.

While we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Location Data

DSMS Mobile uses location services to:

- Track driving lesson routes in real-time.
- Record route history for training purposes.
- Verify lesson locations and meeting points.
- Provide location-based scheduling features.

**Important:** Location tracking is only active during scheduled driving lessons and when explicitly enabled by the user. You can control location permissions through your device settings. Disabling location access may limit certain features of the app.

## Image and Document Access

The app may request access to your camera and photo library to:

- Upload identity documents (driver's license, permits, ID cards).

We only access images and documents when you explicitly choose to upload them. All documents are stored securely and encrypted.
